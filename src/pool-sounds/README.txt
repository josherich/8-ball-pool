POOL GAME SOUND EFFECTS
=======================

Files & Usage:
  ball_collision.mp3       - Quick ball-to-ball hit (0.2s)
  cue_strike.mp3           - Cue stick striking cue ball (0.4s)
  cushion_hit.mp3          - Ball bouncing off cushion/rail (0.2s)
  ball_pocket.mp3          - Ball dropping into pocket (0.5s)
  break_shot_big.mp3       - Opening break, big scatter (1.1s)
  break_shot_small.mp3     - Smaller break shot (2.9s)
  foul_ding.mp3            - Foul/scratch notification tone (2.3s)
